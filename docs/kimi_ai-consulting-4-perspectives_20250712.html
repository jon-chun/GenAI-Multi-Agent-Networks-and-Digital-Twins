The period following the public release of ChatGPT-3 in October 2022 has marked a significant acceleration in the adoption, investment, and perceived economic impact of Artificial Intelligence globally. Global AI Investment and Market Trends

Market Growth Trajectory

The global AI market has experienced unprecedented growth since ChatGPT-3's launch, characterized by a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 35.9%. Projections indicate robust expansion from $391 billion in early 2025 to nearly five times that amount over the subsequent five years [43].

Key Growth Drivers

  • • Generative AI investment surge from $25.2B (2023) to $33.9B (2024) [8]
  • • 40.6% increase in newly funded AI companies, reaching 1,812 in 2023
  • • Global IT spending on AI projected to reach $521B by 2027

Adoption and Investment Landscape

The EU pursues a cautious, regulation-first strategy with its comprehensive AI Act. While private investment declined 14.1% from 2022-2023, European VC firms still invested $5.10 billion in Q1 2025, demonstrating continued commitment to responsible AI development.

Methodological Notes and Data Sources

This whitepaper synthesizes information from leading academic institutions, industry research firms, international organizations, and government agencies. Key sources include Stanford Institute for Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence (HAI) AI Index Reports, McKinsey & Company, PwC, Goldman Sachs, IMF, and World Economic Forum.

Data Limitations and Considerations

  • • Temporal Dynamics: AI field evolves rapidly; data may become outdated quickly
  • • Definitional Variance: Different methodologies for "AI-related jobs" and "AI adoption" across sources
  • • Projection Uncertainty: Economic impact forecasts depend on numerous assumptions
  • • Regional Disparities: Varying data collection standards across countries
  • • Industry Bias: Private research may reflect vested interests or optimistic projections

The analysis employs comparative methods to highlight differences between regions, focusing on developments since ChatGPT-3's launch in October 2022. Data points are prioritized based on recency and relevance to economic impact assessment.
